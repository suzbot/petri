Construction-Reqs.txt

Over-arching story: Characters can gather various materials and construct small buildings out of grass, sticks, or bricks. They will build them based on the resources available and their own preferences. This will support future work where they can store things inside buildings to protect them from other creatures, or sleep inside of buildings for better sleep. For now, they will provide diversion and something to look at and form preferences about.

New Item grouping: Bundle
	- Sticks can be carried or dropped as a stack
	- A stack of sticks is called a bundle. A bundle of sticks can have up to 6 pieces in it.
	- a picked up stack can be added to until it has max pieces

New Item: Tall Grass
	- Spawns at world creation (2x as many as other plant types)
	- Pale Green
	- Growing, reproduces like other plants, fast lifecycle
	- Bears seeds in the same manner as flowers that can be picked up by foraging
	- As a plant, should show up on the harvest list
	- Harvested grass CANNOT be placed in a vessel (too large, may fit in future containers), but can be carried or dropped as a stack
	- A stack of grass is called a bundle. A bundle of grass can have up to 6 pieces in it.
	- a picked up stack can be added to until it has max pieces

New Feature: Clay
	- on world generation, 6-10 tiles on the map are 'clay' tiles (reddish brown pattern)
	- tiles must be adjacent to a water tile
	- tiles must be adjacent to at least one other clay tile
	- are passable

New Action: Gather > Clay
	- loose in hand pick up 1 clay from clay tile (clay tile is infinite source like water source)
	- available by default, does not require know-how
	- orderable

New Activity: Craft > Bricks
	- orderable
	- discoverable chance by picking up or looking at clay
	- user selects number of bricks to craft

New Activity: Construct > Fence
	Recipe: Thatch Fence - 1 bundle of 6 per tile
	Recipe: Stick Fence - 1 bundle of 6 per tile
	Recipe: Brick Fence - 6 bricks per tile

Preference Formation:
 - Characters can look at Construction
 - Characters can form a like/dislike materials in the construction they look at
 	- Thatch
 	- Sticks
 	- Bricks

New Activity: Construct > Hut
	Recipe: Thatch Hut - 2 full bundles per tile x 16 tiles
	Recipe: Stick Hut - 2 full bundles per tile x 16 tiles
	Recipe: Brick Hut - 12 bricks per tile x 16 tiles
	Action - marked tiles for construction get all their supplies dropped on them and then each tile with all its supplies is worked for a duration before worker moves to start next tile
	Outcome - 4x4 outline of tiles around 3x3 opening inside; tiles are not passable except one tile which is a door (only passable by chars)

	Implement similar to tilling where there are tiles marked for building, and you can review selected areas on that screen, and have more than one worker working on it. 

	Make sure order is becomes unavailable or unfulfullable when materials run out, and then can be re-taken when materials come available again

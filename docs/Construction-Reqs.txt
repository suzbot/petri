Construction-Reqs.txt

Over-arching story: Characters can gather various materials and construct small buildings out of grass, sticks, or bricks. They will build them based on the resources available and their own preferences. This will support future work where they can store things inside buildings to protect them from other creatures, or sleep inside of buildings for better sleep. For now, they will provide diversion and something to look at and form preferences about.



## I. Begin by gathering supplies

**Flower Seeds / Flower Cultivation** (Deferred from Gardening phase)

	Deferred from Gardening Phase (was in Feature Set 2). Flower cultivation is cultural/aesthetic, not survival-critical — the core gardening loop works with gourd seeds + plantable berries/mushrooms.

	**The mechanic tension:** Flower seed gathering doesn't fit cleanly into any existing activity archetype:

	- **Foraging** has become food-centric ("go find snacks"). Flower seeds aren't food.
	- **Gather** is about gathering raw materials from the ground. But flower seeds come _from_ an interaction with a living plant, not the ground.
	- Question how do we work in: "interact with a living plant to extract something, without destroying it" — should it be different verb from foraging, gathering, or harvesting (which kills the plant). New verb: Collect? Extract? Glean? How would that be intuitively be different form gather for the user?

	Is this genuinely a fourth pattern without enough examples yet to know the right abstraction? Construction-reqs will want to follow this pattern with grass, where it both has seeds that can be extracted as well as having the plant be directly harvestable itself. Pigment-reqs will want to be able to harvest the whole flower separately from just extracting/collecting the seeds?

	**Original requirement (Gardening-Reqs line 67):** "Foraging a flower produces 1 flower variety seed without removing the flower."

	Options:
	- Glean seeds
	- Collect seeds (confusing with gather gourd seeds)
	- Extract from growing plant (long name, but could allow for more creative things later - sap, essence, etc?)

## Construction

New Concept:
	- Fences and Huts are a new category of thing in this world. They are constructed in place by characters. They can't be picked up. They aren't passable.
	- In future requirements they will have durability based on materials and will be destroyable.
	- In future requirements they may be dismantleable.

New Activity: Construction > Build Fence
	Recipe: Thatch Fence - 1 bundle of 6 per tile
	Recipe: Stick Fence - 1 bundle of 6 per tile
	Recipe: Brick Fence - 6 bricks per tile

## Interaction Richness 

**Activity Preferences**
	Characters form opinions about order categories (Garden, Harvest, Craft) through experience, similar to how food preferences form from eating.

   **Formation:** Chance to form a preference for the order's activity category on order completion, based on mood (same trigger conditions as item preference formation — Joyful/Happy → like, Unhappy/Miserable → dislike). Same formation chance as item preferences initially; can be tuned separately later if needed.

   **Player-visible impact (v1):** Mood change rate during the activity while performing it. A character who likes gardening feels happier while gardening; one who dislikes it feels worse. Visible in the preferences panel as a separate category (like how knowledge panel has Facts and Know-how sections).

   **Preference structure:** Separate category in the preferences panel, not the same Preference struct as item preferences (different target type — category string vs item attributes). Displayed alongside item preferences but visually distinct.

   **Future extensions (not v1):**
   - Preference affects which orders characters voluntarily accept
   - Personality emergence — characters develop work identities over time

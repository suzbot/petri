Phase 5 Requirements - Picking up Items and Inventory

General Idea:
This phase introduces the concept of items that can be picked up, and containers.

New ItemType: Gourd
    1. Gourd belongs to Category Plant
        i. Flowers, Berries, and Mushrooms are also Plants
        ii. Plants are a kind of item that can spawn on the map at world creation, reproduce,
            and potentially die.
        iii. Plants may be edible and poison or healing.
        iv.  Mushrooms, Berries, and Flowers are also Plants
    2. Gourd follows the same spawning and reproduction rules as mushrooms
    3. Gourds are edible.
    4. Gourds are never poison or healing.
    5. Characters can interact with gourds by eating or looking
    6. Gourds can be: white, green, yellow, orange
    7. Gourds can have Pattern striped (stripes) or no pattern
    8. Gourds can have texture: knobbly or no texture
New Idle Activity: Forage

Character has inventory view
    1. When a character is selected, the user has an option to press 'i' for the bottom panel
        to show 'Inventory'
    2. When the inventory is viewed, it will say 'Carrying [item]' for each item that is carried
